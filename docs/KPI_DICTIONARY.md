# KPI Dictionary

This dictionary documents existing KPIs and dashboard indicators only. No new KPI is created in Stage 1B.

| KPI | definition | formula | numerator | denominator | unit | frequency | dimension | interpretation | limitation | source | dashboard page | validation rule |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| poverty rate | Monetary poverty headcount | `monetary_poverty` | population below selected poverty line | total population | percent | annual | country, region, year | Measures poverty incidence | Source definitions and coverage vary | processed panel; WDI; Equity Lab fallback | country profiles; poverty evolution | value should be 0-100 |
| extreme poverty rate | Extreme poverty headcount | `extreme_poverty` | population below extreme poverty line | total population | percent | annual | country, region, year | Measures severe poverty incidence | Source definitions and coverage vary | processed panel; WDI; Equity Lab fallback | country profiles | value should be 0-100 |
| informality rate | Labor informality rate | `labor_informality` | informal employment | employment or source denominator | percent | annual | country, region, year | Labor-market vulnerability signal | Definitions and coverage vary across sources | processed panel; ILOSTAT; Equity Lab fallback | informality evolution; scatter | value should be 0-100 |
| unemployment | Unemployment rate | `unemployment` | unemployed labor force | labor force | percent | annual | country, region, year | Labor-market slack | Missingness and definition differences constrain comparison | processed panel; Equity Lab; ILOSTAT fallback | dashboard country profile | value should be 0-100 |
| employment rate | Employment-to-population ratio | `employment` | employed population | working-age or source population | percent | annual | country, region, year | Employment intensity | Denominator definition is source-specific | processed panel; ILOSTAT | dashboard profile REVIEW_REQUIRED | value should be 0-100 |
| social protection coverage | Population covered by social protection | `social_protection_coverage` | covered population | total population | percent | annual | country, region, year | Coverage of protective systems | High missingness; source coverage uneven | processed panel; ASPIRE; WDI fallback | social protection coverage | value should be 0-100 |
| social insurance coverage | Social insurance coverage | `social_insurance_coverage_aspire` | covered population | reference population | percent | annual | country, year | Formal protection channel | Coverage incomplete and ASPIRE-specific | ASPIRE | REVIEW_REQUIRED | value should be 0-100 |
| social assistance coverage | Social assistance coverage | `social_assistance_coverage_aspire` | covered population | reference population | percent | annual | country, year | Non-contributory protection channel | Coverage incomplete and ASPIRE-specific | ASPIRE | REVIEW_REQUIRED | value should be 0-100 |
| vulnerability index | Structural Vulnerability Index | mean of z-scored poverty, informality, unemployment, Gini, low social protection and low GDP | standardized vulnerability components | available components | z-score | annual | country, region, year | Descriptive multi-domain vulnerability comparison | Not causal; not automatic policy ranking | processed panel | ranking, map, Bolivia view | finite numeric value |
| missingness rate | Share of missing observations by variable | missing values / total rows | missing observations | total rows | percent | panel-level | variable | Identifies coverage constraints | Missingness reflects source coverage and quality | validation report; table 5 | missingness table | value should be 0-100 |
| country coverage | Number of countries and years covered | count distinct country-years | observed country-years | not applicable | count | panel-level | country, year | Indicates panel scope | Does not imply equal indicator coverage | panel build summary; validation report | dashboard overview | duplicate country-year rows should be zero |
| Bolivia versus regional benchmark | Bolivia compared with LAC average or ranking | Bolivia indicator minus regional benchmark, where available | Bolivia value | regional benchmark | indicator-specific | annual | Bolivia, region, year | Positions Bolivia in regional context | Missingness affects comparability | figure 12; ranking table; dashboard | Bolivia profile | benchmark must use same indicator and period |
