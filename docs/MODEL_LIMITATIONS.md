# Model Limitations

## Identification

The model suite estimates policy-relevant associations, not causal impacts. Labor informality and social protection coverage are endogenous to poverty, economic structure, fiscal capacity, and political institutions.

## Inference

The preferred model has fewer than 30 country clusters. Country-clustered inference is still preferred over conventional standard errors, but p-values should be read cautiously.

## Dynamic Panel

Arellano-Bond and System GMM are included because the project brief requested them and because poverty is persistent. However, the current sample produces singular weighting-matrix warnings. These models are robustness checks only.

## Data Coverage

The panel is unbalanced. Informality and social protection coverage jointly constrain the main estimation sample. Bolivia household microdata are available for future validation but not yet fully harmonized into this first cross-country panel.
