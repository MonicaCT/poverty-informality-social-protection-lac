# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, career stage, or technical experience.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and precise in technical disagreement.
- Critique methods, evidence, and code rather than people.
- Acknowledge uncertainty when discussing empirical results.
- Protect confidential data, credentials, and non-public research material.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, discriminatory language, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private data, credentials, or confidential research material.
- Misrepresenting findings, uncertainty, authorship, or source provenance.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions that violate these standards. Serious or repeated violations may result in blocked participation.
