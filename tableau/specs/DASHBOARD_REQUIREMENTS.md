# Dashboard Requirements

Build exactly five Tableau dashboards and one story from public aggregate data.

## Dashboards

1. Regional Executive Overview.
2. Poverty and Inequality.
3. Labor and Informality.
4. Social Protection.
5. Data Quality and Comparability.

## Required Evidence

- Use `tableau/data/` as the only data input package.
- Use country and year as the main dimensions.
- Use documented calculated fields only.
- Keep `REVIEW_REQUIRED` formulas out of final dashboards.
- Show data-quality and comparability notes.
- Treat all results as descriptive, not causal.

## Non-Requirements

- No raw data.
- No new indicators.
- No model estimation.
- No regenerated figures or tables.
- No Tableau Public publication until a real workbook exists and passes QA.
