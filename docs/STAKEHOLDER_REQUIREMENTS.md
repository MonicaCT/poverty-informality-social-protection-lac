# Stakeholder Requirements

This document maps existing project indicators to stakeholder decisions. It does not introduce new indicators.

| stakeholder | decision | information need | KPI | visual | frequency | level of detail | risk of misinterpretation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ministries of social development | target social-protection priorities | poverty, extreme poverty, coverage and missingness by country-year | poverty rate; extreme poverty rate; social protection coverage | country profile, coverage chart, missingness table | annual | country-year, region | Treating descriptive gaps as causal program impacts. |
| labor ministries | understand informality and labor-market vulnerability | labor informality, unemployment, employment and gender participation | informality rate; unemployment; employment rate | informality trend, scatter, profile table | annual | country-year | Comparing informality without noting source definition differences. |
| social protection agencies | monitor protection coverage and adequacy | coverage, social assistance, insurance, pensions and cash transfer variables | social protection coverage; social insurance coverage; social assistance coverage | coverage chart and profile | annual | country-year | Assuming missing coverage means zero coverage. |
| multilateral organizations | compare regional vulnerability and data gaps | cross-country poverty, informality, coverage, vulnerability and missingness | vulnerability index; country coverage; missingness rate | ranking, regional map, regional summary | annual or project cycle | country, region | Reading rankings as causal or normative ordering. |
| research teams | identify model-ready samples and limitations | complete-case sample, lags, interaction terms, source lineage | country coverage; missingness rate; vulnerability index | validation tables and metadata | project update | variable, country-year | Ignoring complete-case shrinkage and missingness mechanisms. |
| country program managers | position one country against regional patterns | country profile, Bolivia comparison, benchmark figures | Bolivia versus regional benchmark; poverty rate; informality rate | Bolivia vs LAC chart and dashboard profile | annual | country and region | Overinterpreting one-year rankings or missing comparator data. |

## Communication Requirements

- Always identify indicator source and coverage.
- Mark missingness as a substantive data-quality result.
- Distinguish descriptive monitoring from causal evaluation.
- Keep social-protection interpretation cautious because ASPIRE and WDI coverage differ.
- Keep informality interpretation cautious because definitions and source availability vary.
